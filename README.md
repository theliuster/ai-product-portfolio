# AI Product Portfolio

This repository will house my AI product management projects as I build hands-on experience with LLMs, RAG, agents, evaluation, and applied AI workflows.

## Goal

My goal is to become a stronger AI Product Manager by building real AI products, documenting product and technical decisions, and creating portfolio-ready examples that demonstrate practical AI fluency.

## Planned Projects

### 1. AI PRD Generator
An AI-powered tool that turns messy product inputs into structured PRDs, user stories, acceptance criteria, risks, and open questions.

### 2. Customer Feedback Intelligence
A RAG-based tool that analyzes customer feedback, identifies recurring themes, summarizes pain points, and cites source examples.

### 3. Banking Copilot
A flagship AI assistant concept for digital banking support that uses RAG, tool calling, escalation logic, guardrails, and evaluation.

### 4. Multi-Agent Product Team
A multi-agent workflow that simulates product, research, engineering, and QA perspectives to generate stronger product briefs.

### 5. Prompt Evaluation Dashboard
A tool for comparing prompt variants across quality, latency, token cost, and failure cases.
